About

What is Snowmania?

Well, you see, it's an adventure. Not just any adventure though, a snowman themed adventure. Snowmania is a scavenger hunt out in the woods of Oregon, where participants try to find snowmen and solve/follow the clues/questions found within those (->)

snowmen, to lead themselves to the ultimate jackput of 25 grand ($25,000). All during the one of the most favorable times of year, christmas! Snowmania is filled with different challenges to spice up the game along the way. It's filled with choas, puzzels, problem solving, and most importantly, fun!

Where? When?

Snowmania has partnered with the Deschutes National Forest in Oregon to bring the competition to the west cost during the week of January 5, 2020. (1/5/2020 - 1/11/2020)

Cost?

Yeah, your probably thinking: "A competition like this has got to cost a lot, right?" Well, the base price to attend is actually $0. However, this doesn't include prices for hotels, etc.

Prizes?

To encourage the competitive side of the competition, we will be only offering one prize this year (where previous year's we've offered 5.) with a grand total of 25 grand. ($25,000)

Explore

Seize the opportunity to explore the vast forests of Oregon, and see amazing sights, and have an overall amazing experience.

The Hunt

Locate different snowmen around the Deschutes National Forest to find clues and puzzels, to ultimately lead yourself to victory.

FAMILY

Snowmania provides families with a fun christmas vacation, but it also provides teamwork and bonding experience with those close to you.

Policies

Every competition has it's policies/rules. So here's some of the more important ones, a full list will be emailed before the event. Failure to obide by these policies will result in disqualification from the competition.

1. Team sizes must be between 3-10 people.

2. Working with other teams is not allowed.

3. If your team wins, you may choose what you wish to do with the prize money afterwards.

4. You may not sign up once the competition has started.

5. Team members have permission to quit during the competition. We don't "force" you to do anything.

6. Exploring beyond the competition boundries will result in disqualification.

7. The most important rule of them all, make sure to have fun!
